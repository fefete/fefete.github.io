Unnatural Guardian UE4 Game

Summary

Beautiful looking game made for a module in Teesside university in UE4, made in a group of artists, programmers and designers. This game is an storytelling game that consists mostly in enviromental setup and feeling, having to take good care of the look and feel of the game. I worked mostly in the player setup and feeling, also, with the enviromental events and sound system, ensuring that everything met the quality of the team leader, maintaining the look and feel of the game and the player immersion

Techniques used and technical info

  • C++
  • Blueprint
  • AI UE4 module programming
  • UE4 animations
  • UE4 sound system
